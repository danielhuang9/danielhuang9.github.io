Sports

Soccer

I began playing soccer at a young age because my parents wanted me to be active. I remember stepping on the field for the first time and just knowing that I would love this sport. As I played for longer, I became more invested and decided to play competitively Waterloo. Playing at a high level taught me to work well in a team and understand the importance of relationships. Everything I know about working together in a team today, I owe to soccer.

Gaming

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive was the first competitive game I got in to. I have always been a competitive person and these habits transferred well into CSGO. I would spend hours weekly studying the game and practicing my mechanics. With this dedication, I was able to reach the highest rank in the game, Global Elite.

Valorant

After 4+ years of playing CSGO, I felt accomplished as I reached my peak at the highest rank. Another similar game, Valorant, came along and I was hooked right when I started. It was a great change of scenery as CSGO became stale to me. Valorant could not have come at a better time. Most skills from CSGO are applicable to Valorant and I was able to reach the second highest rank, Immortal.
